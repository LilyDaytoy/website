Research

I am deeply fascinated by the capabilities and potential of generative models. My current research focuses on developing highly controllable and compositional generative models that empower creative and interactive content creation and manipulation. I aspire to to harness AI’s imagination to aid innovative art design. Additionally, I’m also interested in multi-modalities research and advancements in vision-language models and visual reasoning tasks.
